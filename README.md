# Myntra Sales Dashboard – Key Insights

# Business Performance
• Total Sales reached ₹1.02M with 364 total orders and an Average Order Value of ₹2.80K.
• Overall customer satisfaction is strong with an average rating of 3.98 from 0.72M ratings.

# Time-Based Trends
• January recorded peak monthly sales (~₹710K), followed by a gradual dip until May, then a slight recovery in June.
• Sunday generated the highest daily sales (~₹666K), while Tuesday showed the lowest (~₹440K).
• Weekly trend shows major spikes around Week 27, indicating campaign or seasonal sale impact.

# Quarterly Performance
• Q1 was the strongest quarter with ₹2.09M revenue and 754 orders.
• Sales declined slightly in Q2 and Q3, but ratings improved to 4.1 in Q3, suggesting better product satisfaction despite lower sales.
